Medical Programs

Pathways Designed for You

Every aspiring physician is different. We offer multiple entry points into medicine to match where you are today.

4 Years
Doctor of Medicine (MD)
Five Basic Science semesters followed by clinical rotations at affiliated hospitals in the U.S. and Canada. Students sit the USMLE Step 1 with faculty support.
Master's Program
Pre-Medical Master's
For career changers with a bachelor's degree. Earn a Master's in Biomedical Science and gain direct entry consideration into MD programs.
5 Years
5-Year BSc/MD Program
One pre-med year before the 4-year MD. For students with A-Levels, IB diplomas, or equivalent. No MCAT required.
6 Years
6-Year BSc/MD Program
Two-year pre-med curriculum then the full MD — on-campus or online. Perfect for secondary school graduates committed to medicine.
15 Weeks
Gateway Medical Program
A clinical sciences bridge for students with conditional MD acceptance. Full-time or part-time, in-person or online.
Transfer Pathway
MBBS to MD Pathway
Hold an MBBS? Seek transfer admission into the MD program with advanced standing and practise medicine in North America.
